Welcome to Prickly Pair Caravan

What are you?

Prickly Pair Caravan is a way for you to try out games at a low cost. FREE!!

Before tarrifs, there was an estimated >3000 games being published every year. That's a lot of games! And not all of them are good or a fit for you. So I curate a good selection for you to try out

Prickly Pair Caravan does gamenight:
Tuesdays @ Quarter Celtic Taproom 6pm-8pm
Wednesdays @ Quarter Celtic Brewpub 6pm-8pm

You don't have to end your game right at 8, both locations are open until 9 but it is a good idea to begin winding down
