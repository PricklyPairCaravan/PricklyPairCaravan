MañanaCon

MañanaCon is New Mexico's only gaming convention for tabletop gamers of all kinds. We have everything from 10-minute, bite-sized card games to marathon games and tournaments that last a whole day or more. So come join us for four days of gaming, meeting old friends and making new ones, and generally having a great time relaxing and letting your troubles melt away.

Bubonicon

Bubonicon is New Mexico's best and longest-running Sci-Fi & Fantasy literary and arts convention. Join us in Albuquerque for a great weekend of fun! We have panels and presentations (featuring top authors, artists, and scientists), a costume contest, art show, dealer's room, Green Slime Awards, charity auctions, fan programming, and much more!
