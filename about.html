Prickly Pair Caravan

About me

What is Prickly Pair Caravan?

Prickly Pair Caravan is a way for you to try new board games at a low cost. If you don't like the game then hey, you only spent $15 or less.

What inspired you to make the cart?

Imitation is the highest form of flattery. I saw a postpost of someone else who did something similar. They converted a pedal ice cream cart to sell board games out of. That combined with my love of board games gave me the motivation to make Prickly Pair Caravan a reality!

How did you get into board games?

I got into board games at the ripe age of 26 when a coworker invited me to their board game night. I had only ever played the mainstream board games and had no idea there was anything beyond that. From there the seed was planted and I hope Prickly Pair Caravan brings you some of that joy as well!

How do you get your prices so low?

A mixture of sales, discount codes, and meeting free shipping requirements allows me to sell games at or below $15.

What are your margins?

I believe the most profit I make of a game is 20% with many games being around 5%-10% after accounting for tax. Combined with how much time I spend looking for games really makes this a labor of love. I'm not quitting my day job anytime soon ha.

How did you build the cart?

I purchased these design plans for a cotton candy cart and then made modifications of my own. The biggest being wheelbarrow tires instead of bicycle due to the amount of goatheads here.

Plans for the future?

Would love to be outside other breweries/coffe shops. Maybe some events like WineFest or Summerfests but we'll take it slow. No plans whatsoever for a physical location, I think we'll always stay a cart/pop-up.
