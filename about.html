What is Prickly Pair Caravan

My goal is to get people into boardgames beyond the usual Monopoly, Clue, etc. And I feel a big obstacle to that is price. There are a lot of boardgames out there and it's a big ask to spend 20, 30, 40 dollars on a game that you may not like.
